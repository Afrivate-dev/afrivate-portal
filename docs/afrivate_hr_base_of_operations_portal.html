Foundation · The Shift

HR is not support. HR is strategy.

The original Base of Operations described what world-class People & Culture looks like. This edition describes how AfriVate runs it — with the portal as the system of record, not Google Forms and Sheets.

💡
The AfriVate HR MandateHR exists to attract the right people, develop them relentlessly, connect them to purpose, and build culture that sustains performance. Admin, compliance, and payroll are infrastructure — not the mission.
🖥
The portal principleNeeds a record, approval, or audit trail → portal. Needs inbox reach → Gmail. Teaches a skill → Alison. Master document → Drive (with a portal copy for staff).

Section 3 · The 8 Pillars · Portal Implementation

What HR does and where to do it

Each pillar includes portal location, external tool, owner, and a step-by-step SOP.

🎯
1 · Talent Acquisition
Structured, values-aligned hiring
  • Open requisitions → Admin → HR dashboard
  • Candidate pipeline: Applied → Hired
  • Job posts → LinkedIn / Instagram
  • Interviews → Meet + guide in Drive
SOP · New hire pipeline
  1. Create requisition in HR dashboard
  2. Post on LinkedIn; applications to hr@afrivate.org
  3. Track candidates through stages in dashboard
  4. On Hired: invite to portal, set department + reports-to
🚀
2 · Onboarding
30-60-90 integration
  • Videos + checklist → /onboarding
  • Milestones → /people/growth Milestones
  • Day 7/30/90 1:1s → Growth → 1:1 + Meet
  • Day 90 survey → Surveys
SOP · First 90 days
  1. HR approves user, assigns dept/team/reports-to
  2. Day 1: complete Getting started
  3. Milestones auto-seed on first Growth visit
  4. Manager logs 1:1s monthly; HR runs Day 90 pulse
📊
3 · Performance
OKRs, 1:1s, 360° — not annual theatre
  • Quarterly OKRs → Growth → OKRs
  • Monthly 1:1s → Growth → 1:1 (toggle completed)
  • Bi-annual 360° → Growth → 360°
  • PIP templates → Drive + IDP in portal
SOP · Monthly 1:1
  1. Manager schedules Meet with each report
  2. After meeting: mark completed in Growth → 1:1
  3. HR dashboard tracks 1:1 completion rate KPI
📚
4 · Learning & Development
Alison + portal submissions
  • Assign course → Admin HR dashboard
  • Announce → Gmail Course of the Month
  • Complete → Alison.com
  • Submit cert → /people/learning
  • IDP → Growth → IDP (bi-annual)
SOP · Monthly L&D cycle
  1. Day 1: assign course in HR dashboard
  2. Send Gmail with Alison link + portal submission reminder
  3. HR approves certificates; celebrate in digest
💬
5 · Engagement
Listen, recognise, communicate
  • HR digest → Gmail + Memos (type: HR digest)
  • Pulse / eNPS → Surveys
  • Shout-outs + AfriVate Awards → People hub
  • Town hall → Events + Meet
  • Grievances + exit interviews → Growth / Admin
SOP · Bi-weekly digest
  1. Draft content (spotlight, course, celebrations)
  2. Send Gmail to all staff
  3. Post same content: Memos → type HR digest
💰
6 · Compensation
External until HRIS scale
  • Salary bands → Drive (confidential Sheet)
  • Annual review → HR + CEO via Gmail
  • OKR-linked bonus talks → Growth OKRs + 1:1
  • Leave benefit → /people/leave
Not in portal

Payroll and pay amounts stay in Finance systems. Portal links performance (OKRs) to conversations, not payroll execution.

🌱
7 · Wellbeing & Inclusion
Visible in listening channels
  • Wellbeing questions in monthly pulse
  • Flexible leave via portal
  • Safe reporting → Grievance tab + hr@
  • December wellbeing course → Alison + Learning
📋
8 · HR Operations
Records, policies, reporting
  • Employee records → Admin + Directory
  • Policies → Resources + acknowledgment
  • Contracts → Drive (signed PDFs)
  • Monthly HR report → Admin KPIs
SOP · Publish policy
  1. Finalize master in Drive
  2. Upload to Resources; enable Requires acknowledgment
  3. Post Memo type Policy notice
  4. Track acks until 100% within 30 days

Section 7 · Standard Workflows

Quick-reference playbooks

A · New employee (week 1)
  1. Admin invites → approve → set dept + reports-to
  2. Employee completes Getting started
  3. HR assigns Month 1 course + welcome Memo
  4. Manager schedules Day 7 1:1; verify milestones in Growth
B · Monthly HR cycle (~2 hrs)
  1. Assign course in HR dashboard
  2. Send Gmail course email
  3. Ensure pulse survey active
  4. Publish digest (Gmail + Memo digest type)
  5. Approve learning submissions
  6. Review KPIs; email CEO summary
C · Quarterly awards
  1. Collect nominations (shout-outs or email)
  2. Select winners: Innovation, Team Spirit, Most Improved, Embodied the Way
  3. Record in Admin → HR dashboard
  4. Feature in digest + town hall
D · Departure
  1. Log exit interview in HR dashboard
  2. Deactivate portal account on last day
  3. Archive Drive access per policy

When scaling

Production setup checklist

  • All SQL migrations applied (through 20260704_hr_operations.sql)
  • .env: VITE_USE_SUPABASE_AUTH=true and VITE_USE_SUPABASE_DATA=true
  • Edge Functions deployed (invite, request-access, admin-patch-profile)
  • Supabase Auth URLs set for portal.afrivate.org
  • HR/admin users promoted in Supabase
  • Departments and teams configured in Admin
  • First policies uploaded with Requires acknowledgment
  • First pulse survey and Alison assignment created
  • Optional: Google Drive picker env vars configured

Success definition — HR at full power

  1. Every employee logs into the portal weekly
  2. Bi-weekly digest goes out on Gmail and as HR digest memos
  3. Monthly pulse achieves >70% response rate
  4. Monthly Alison course achieves >70% approved completion
  5. Managers hit 90% 1:1 completion logged in Growth
  6. Quarterly OKRs exist for every active employee
  7. Policies acknowledged by 100% of staff within 30 days
  8. HR dashboard reviewed monthly and shared with CEO
  9. No duplicate tracking in Google Forms for portal data
